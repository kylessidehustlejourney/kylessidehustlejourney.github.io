Crockpot Chicken Parmesan Pasta

A simple, comforting crockpot meal loaded with tender chicken, rich marinara sauce, pasta, and plenty of melted cheese. Perfect for busy weeknights with minimal prep and maximum flavor.

Watch the YouTube short: Crockpot Chicken Parmesan Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 jar marinara sauce, 24 oz
  • 16 oz pasta, cooked — penne, rotini, or your favorite
  • 8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan or Asiago cheese
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Garlic powder, to taste
  • Italian seasoning, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken breasts into the crockpot.
  2. Season the chicken with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and Italian seasoning.
  3. Pour the marinara sauce over the chicken, making sure it is well covered.
Cook Times:
  • High: 3–4 hours
  • Low: 5–6 hours
Cook until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F.
  1. Remove the chicken and shred it using two forks.
  2. Return the shredded chicken to the crockpot and stir it into the sauce.
  3. While the chicken finishes cooking, prepare the pasta according to the package directions. Drain well.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the crockpot and stir until evenly coated with sauce.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella and Parmesan or Asiago evenly over the top.
  6. Cover the crockpot for 5–10 minutes, allowing the residual heat to melt the cheese.
  7. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley and serve warm.

Optional Additions

  • Crushed red pepper flakes for a little heat
  • Sautéed mushrooms
  • Fresh spinach
  • Diced onions
  • Minced garlic
  • Extra Parmesan cheese for serving
  • Garlic bread on the side
  • Caesar salad for a complete meal
Serving Tip: This dish is cozy, cheesy, and perfect with warm garlic bread or a crisp Caesar salad.

Enjoy!
