Crockpot Chicken Parmesan Pasta

A simple slow-cooker version of chicken parmesan mixed with pasta — great for a hands-off dinner.

Watch the short: Crockpot Chicken Parmesan Pasta

Quick notes

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 3-4 hours on high (or 6-8 hours on low)

Ingredients (basic)

  • Chicken breasts
  • Marinara sauce
  • Pasta (penne or rotini)
  • Mozzarella & parmesan

Instructions (summary)

  1. Place chicken and sauce in crockpot; cook until tender.
  2. Shred chicken, stir in cooked pasta and cheeses.
  3. Serve hot, garnish with basil.
