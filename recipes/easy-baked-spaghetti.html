Easy Baked Spaghetti

A quick baked spaghetti recipe that's comforting and simple — see the short for the steps.

Watch the short: Easy Baked Spaghetti

Quick notes

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 20-25 minutes

Ingredients (basic)

  • Spaghetti or broken pasta
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ground beef or sausage (optional)
  • Mozzarella & parmesan

Instructions (summary)

  1. Cook pasta to al dente.
  2. Mix with sauce and meat (if using), top with cheese and bake until bubbly.
