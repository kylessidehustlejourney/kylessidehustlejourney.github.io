🍝 Easy Baked Spaghetti

A simple, cheesy baked spaghetti recipe that's perfect for busy weeknights. Creamy Alfredo sauce, hearty meat sauce, and melted mozzarella come together for an easy family favorite.

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz) package spaghetti noodles
  • 1 (15 oz) jar Alfredo sauce
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 (24 oz) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 2–3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook the spaghetti according to the package directions until al dente. Drain well.
  3. Spread the entire jar of Alfredo sauce evenly across the bottom of a 9×13-inch baking dish.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti noodles and gently stir until all the noodles are coated with the Alfredo sauce.
  5. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain any excess grease.
  6. Stir the spaghetti sauce into the cooked ground beef and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  7. Spread the meat sauce evenly over the Alfredo-coated spaghetti. Do not stir the layers together.
  8. Top with the shredded mozzarella cheese.
🔥 Bake
  • Bake for 30 minutes.
  • The cheese should be melted, bubbly, and lightly golden.
  • Allow the casserole to rest for 5–10 minutes before serving.

✨ Optional Additions

  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning
  • Garlic powder
  • Fresh chopped parsley
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Diced onions
  • Pepperoni for a fun pizza-inspired version
Serving Tip: Pair this baked spaghetti with garlic bread, Caesar salad, or roasted vegetables for an easy and satisfying family dinner.

🍽️ Enjoy!
